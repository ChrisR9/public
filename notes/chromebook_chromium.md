## Chromebook Keystrokes ##
Once you put the Chromebook into developer mode, you have a few boot options
that you can take;
- To boot Chromium: `Ctrl-D`
- To boot off of the USB/MMC: `Ctrl-U`

To switch to a developer console in Chromium:
- `Ctrl+Alt+Forward`
